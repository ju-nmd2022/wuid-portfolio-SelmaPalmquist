ABOUT ME


I am a 20 year old girl from Sweden and I am currently studying New Media Design at Jönköping University. Since I began studying this program autumn 2022 I will graduate in June 2025, after which I hope to continue with a creative profession.

I have been painting and drawing on and off my whole life and have over the course of my life made living room paintings for the Swedish SoMe-profile Filip Dikmen, designed merch and am currently working on a poster for a small production movie. Furthermore I work with commissions I receive from my instagram page @selma_drawingss and, of course, paint for fun when I feel inspired.

My drawing style mainly consists of realism (aiming for hyperrealism) and I often include some sort of portrait. I feel comfortable drawing in any style though, and have tried branching out to try something more expressive and abstract. Most often I use pen on paper or draw digitally with my iPad and an Apple Pen. Lately I have tried working with oil and acrylic paint/acrylic markers on canvas and have done a few orders with these mediums as well.
